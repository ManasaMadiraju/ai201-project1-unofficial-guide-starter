SOURCE: Princeton Review — Santa Clara University Campus Profile
URL: https://www.princetonreview.com/college/santa-clara-university-1023579

ACADEMICS:
- Academic Rating: 83/99 | Professor Accessibility: 92/99
- Student-to-Faculty Ratio: 11:1 | 4-year Graduation Rate: 81%
- Students describe the academic environment as "rigorous but rewarding"
- Professors are "accessible" and maintain "real relationships" with students
- "I have had a real relationship with every one of my professors at SCU."
- Small class sizes throughout — this is one of SCU's most consistent strengths
- Business school praised for "massive array of classes" with faculty who are "CEOs and founders"
- Top-tier programs: accounting, computer science
- "State-of-the-art equipment" in science facilities

STUDENT BODY:
- 49% female, 51% male | 42% out-of-state students
- "Smart, dedicated, funny" and "intelligent"
- Community balances academics with social life: "people like to have fun, but only after work is finished"
- 75% of students receive some form of financial aid
- Campus described as "overwhelmingly upper-middle class" in demographic feel

CAMPUS LIFE:
- Quality of Life Rating: 84/99 | Fire Safety: 96/99 | Green Rating: 96/99
- 187 registered student organizations
- 30% of students participate in intramural sports
- Greek life (fraternities and sororities) is "huge" and "everywhere" — significant part of social scene
- "Big party culture" on weekends, particularly around Greek life
- Close to San Francisco (~45 min), Santa Cruz beaches (~30 min), and Bay Area hiking
- Silicon Valley location provides unparalleled internship and networking opportunities

HOUSING:
- 49% of all undergrads live on campus
- 91% of first-year students are housed on-campus (effectively required for freshmen)

ADMISSIONS SNAPSHOT:
- Acceptance Rate: 48% | Average HS GPA: 3.72
- SAT Range: 1360–1410 | ACT Range: 31–33 | Test Optional policy in effect

COST:
- Tuition 2025: ~$61,000/year (Engineering: ~$60,000–65,000/year)
- Total with housing: ~$80,000+/year
