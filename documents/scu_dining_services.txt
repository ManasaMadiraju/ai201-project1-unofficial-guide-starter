DINING LOCATIONS ON CAMPUS:

The Marketplace (Benson Memorial Center) — main all-you-can-eat dining hall. Managed by Bon Appétit, emphasizing made-from-scratch, seasonal, and locally sourced ingredients. Recently renovated and described by students as "more like a professional dining cafeteria, similar to Silicon Valley company dining." Rated 3.4/5 for food on Rate My Professors school overview.

Mission Bakery (Benson Memorial Center) — coffee, pastries, grab-and-go options.

Cadence Cyber Cafe (Lucas Hall, Business School) — coffee and snacks for business students.

Fresh Bytes (SCDI / Engineering building) — quick meals and snacks near the engineering school.

Sidebar Cafe (Charney Hall, Law School) — coffee and light meals for law students.

Sunstream Cafe (Learning Commons / Orradre Library) — coffee and snacks for students studying in the library area.

The Cellar Market (Benson Memorial Center) — on-campus grocery store. Students consistently note it is "a bit pricey" compared to off-campus grocery stores.

WEDNESDAY FOOD TRUCKS (10 AM–2 PM, North Dining Plaza):
Aki-ta Sushi, Grillzilla Truck, Oaxacan Kitchen, Fire and Rice, Chick N Bros
These rotate but food trucks appear every Wednesday during the academic year.

ALLERGEN-FRIENDLY:
SimplyOasis station at Benson — designed to avoid the top 9 allergens plus gluten. However, student reports indicate it is not fully reliable for celiac disease management (frequently lists dairy, unclear gluten status).

OFF-CAMPUS PARTNERS (accepting SCU Flex dollars):
Akita Sushi, Cramer's Bagels, Fuel Good, Home Eat, Ike's Sandwiches (gluten-free bread available), Kirk's Steakburgers, OMG Tea, Pizza My Heart (gluten-free pizza), Safeway

STUDENT TIPS:
- Benson is the main dining hall — it's all-you-can-eat and the most economical use of dining points
- The Cellar Market is convenient but expensive for groceries; Safeway (accepts Flex) is cheaper
- Food trucks on Wednesdays are popular and offer variety
- Dining points expire (with limited carryover rules) — plan usage accordingly
