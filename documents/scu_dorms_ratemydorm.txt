SOURCE: RateMyDorm — SCU Freshman Dorms Ranked
URL: https://www.ratemydorm.com/freshman-dorms-ranked/santa-clara-university

96% of SCU freshmen live on campus. Here is how freshman dorms rank based on student reviews:

#1 CAMPISI HALL (Rating: 3.9/5, 3 reviews)
- Ideal for students who prefer quiet study environments
- "Rooms are fairly spacious with 2 large dressers"
- Positioned near the library and gym — convenient for academics and fitness
- Bathrooms are a weak point and need improvement
- Limited social atmosphere — quieter culture than other dorms
- Best for: students who prioritize academics over social life

#2 FINN HALL (Rating: 4.3/5, 1 review)
- Newest residence hall on campus
- Spacious rooms with large closets
- "One of the quieter dorms on campus"
- Farthest from dining facilities, though students don't consider the walk problematic
- Best for: students who want newer facilities and a quieter environment

#3 GRAHAM HALL (Rating: 5/5, 1 review)
- Suite-style rooms: doubles and triples sharing bathroom access within the suite
- "Spacious common rooms" with TVs, study areas, and foosball tables
- Social atmosphere varies significantly by floor
- Best for: students who want suite-style living with shared amenities

#4 SOBRATO HALL (Rating: 4/5, 4 reviews)
- Suite-style hall divided into front and back buildings
- Front building has: common kitchen, game room, media room
- Main issues: "Insufficient washers and dryers" requiring creative scheduling
- Laundry tip: students recommend doing laundry on off-peak hours (early morning or late night)
- Introductory events helped foster meaningful friendships among residents
- Requires proactive cleaning since six students share a suite
- Best for: students who want a social environment with shared kitchen access

GENERAL FRESHMAN HOUSING NOTES:
- Nearly all freshmen (96%) live on campus — this is effectively mandatory
- Room assignments are made before you arrive; you cannot tour rooms beforehand
- Roommate matching can be requested (similar sleep schedule, study habits, etc.)
- On-campus housing costs ~$13,806–15,561/year for room alone (2025-26 rates)
- Most freshmen dorms have RAs (Resident Advisors) who organize floor events and enforce quiet hours
