SOURCE: Spokin Campus Guide — Santa Clara University Food Allergy Information
URL: https://www.spokin.com/food-allergy-friendly-campus-guide-santa-clara-university
Student Profile: Maria Kraus, Allergies: Eggs, Wheat, Sesame (Class of 2020)

OVERALL ASSESSMENT:
"Santa Clara University's staff located in Benson Memorial dining facility has made it very easy for me to enjoy food on campus." — More allergy-friendly options than expected for a campus dining environment.

RECOMMENDED DINING STATIONS AT BENSON (allergy-friendly):
- Saute station — good options for egg/wheat-free meals
- La Parilla — Mexican-style, often has allergy-friendly options
- Fresco — fresh and lighter options
- California Deli — customizable sandwiches where you can control ingredients
- The Bronco — reliable for allergen control

STATIONS TO APPROACH WITH CAUTION:
- SimplyOasis (opened 2023) — officially designed to avoid top-9 allergens plus gluten, but student reports indicate unreliable labeling (frequently lists dairy, unclear about gluten cross-contamination). Students with celiac disease should verify with staff before eating here.

OFF-CAMPUS OPTIONS (allergy-friendly + some accept SCU Flex dollars):
- Pizza My Heart (700 Bellomy St, Santa Clara) — gluten-free pizza available; accepts SCU Flex points
- Pizza Antica (334 Santana Row, San Jose) — quality gluten-free pizza options
- Ike's Love & Sandwiches (75 E Santa Clara St, San Jose) — gluten-free bread available; accepts SCU Flex
- True Food Kitchen (180 El Camino Real, Palo Alto) — specifically designed for food allergy-sensitive diners; extensive allergen information

TIPS FOR STUDENTS WITH FOOD ALLERGIES AT SCU:
- Talk directly to Bon Appétit staff at Benson — they are trained and usually helpful with allergen questions
- The Simply Oasis station is not fully reliable for serious gluten conditions; always double-check
- Use Flex dollars at off-campus partners like Pizza My Heart and Ike's for more allergy-friendly variety
- True Food Kitchen in Palo Alto (accessible by Caltrain or car) is the gold standard for allergy-safe dining near SCU
- Contact SCU Dining Services at the start of the year to register dietary needs — they can sometimes accommodate special requests
