SOURCE: Appily (formerly Cappex) — Santa Clara University Student Reviews
URL: https://www.appily.com/colleges/santa-clara-university/reviews

REVIEW 1 — Logan (CA):
"I can't imagine myself anywhere other than Santa Clara. Kind people, beautiful weather..." Highly positive overall — describes SCU as a dream school for its community and environment.

REVIEW 2 — Kelly (Denver):
"SCU has a beautiful campus with beautiful people. Go here if you're looking for a casual school..." Appreciates the campus beauty and academics but notes it is not a top-tier research institution. Recommends checking fit before applying.

REVIEW 3 — Nicole:
"Wonderful school! I would not attend any other university. Santa Clara offers a plethora of resources..." Extremely positive — highlights the abundance of academic and student support resources available on campus.

REVIEW 4 — Raymond (San Jose, CA):
"Santa Clara university is a school where staff and faculty care about you. The school focuses on enriching both the mind and spirit..." Praises faculty dedication and SCU's Jesuit holistic educational philosophy (cura personalis — care for the whole person).

REVIEW 5 — Anna (Sunnyvale, CA):
Commends the university's commitment to depth of knowledge and a well-rounded education. Notes high professor quality and accessibility.

REVIEW 6 — Stephanie (Los Gatos, CA):
Highlights professor engagement in class. Notes coursework is rigorous and moves at a fast pace — the quarter system means material covers quickly.

REVIEW 7 — Celina (San Jose, CA):
Praises personalized professor attention. Notes that classes are not impacted (overcrowded) the way they can be at larger UC schools — you can generally get into the classes you need.

REVIEW 8 — Deni (CA):
Values professor expertise but critiques unit limitations (SCU has a maximum unit load per quarter) and unpredictable course scheduling — some classes only offered once a year.

REVIEW 9 — Mark (Castro Valley, CA):
Discusses the adjustment to SCU's quarter system. The pace is faster than semester schools — there is little buffer time if you fall behind. Recommends getting ahead early in each quarter.

REVIEW 10 — Bao (San Jose, CA):
Emphasizes faculty expertise and the diversity of student backgrounds, which enhances classroom discussion and learning.

COMMON THEMES FROM STUDENT REVIEWS:
- Beautiful campus, great weather
- Faculty genuinely care and are accessible
- Quarter system pace is fast — don't fall behind
- Classes are not impacted; you can usually get what you need
- Strong Jesuit mission (whole-person education) is felt in daily campus life
- Resources are abundant if you seek them out
